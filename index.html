About me

I am a PhD candidate at the Computer Science Unit, GSSI. Previously, I earned my MSc degree from Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG) and BS degree from Federal University of Ouro Preto (UFOP), both in Computer Science.I also studied for one year at Porto University, Porto, Portugal, during a study abroad program sponsored by the Brazilian government during my undergraduate. Also, I have worked as a Professor at UFOP, University of Itauna (UIT) and University of the State of Minas Gerais (UEMG) from 2016-2020. My research interests include Software Engineering, Software Testing and Self-adaptive Systems.

You can reach me at samira.silva@gssi.it
